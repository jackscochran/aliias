BACKGROUND

So what is a quantitative algorithm?

Quantitative investing is an approach that uses math and statistics to help create models of data which helps train our algorithm. For example the algorithm will look at a companies financials versus another in the market to judge if it is a good idea to buy the company, based on each positive or negative outcome that occurs. Then the algorithm learns and improves its outcome each time. Simply put it takes thousands of data points, picks stocks and improves it outcomes.
