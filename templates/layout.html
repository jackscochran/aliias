About

We use quantitative analysis and AI to rate different North American Stocks on a scale from 1-10. From this ongoing analysis, we've created a portfolio from which members of our community can monitor and track our 25 highest-rated stocks.

We've also created a search feature for members that allows them to search over 1000 stocks (expanding all the time) to acquire insights on the rating we've assigned them. We also include their financials in the context of our ratings.

We are building additional portfolios based on unique investor needs, e.g emerging tech, social responsibility, etc.
